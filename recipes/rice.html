Rice with Rice Cooker

Description

Rice is a staple food in the Philippines and other Asian and African countries. This recipe will show you my way on how to cook rice using a rice cooker.

Ingredients

  • Rice grain
  • Water

Steps

  1. Add your desired amount of rice grains in a pot.
  2. Wash the rice grains in lukewarm water.
  3. Drain the water.
  4. Repeat steps 2 and 3 for two to three times.
  5. Add water until the water is above the top of the rice grains.
  6. Add more water until the water level above the rice grains is at least 1/3 of your middle finger.
  7. Put the pot in the rice cooker and turn it to cook.
  8. Wait until it finished cooking and enjoy!
