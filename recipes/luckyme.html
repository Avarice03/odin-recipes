Lucky Me Pancit Canton

Description

Lucky Me Pancit Canton is a popular instant noodle here in the Philippines. It is easy and quick to cook while tasting great at the same time. I think that it is the only "dish" I can cook by myself (but I'm good with it!).

Warning: I recommend that you should only eat instant noodles once or twice a month if you care about your health.

Ingredients

  • 1 Pack Lucky Me Pancit Canton (Any Flavor)
  • Water
  • Egg

Steps

  1. First, put egg in boiling water for some time.
    • Soft Boiled: 4 to 6 minutes
    • Hard Boiled: 6 to 12 minutes
  2. Remove the egg from the boiling water and put it in a bowl of lukewarm water.
  3. Cook the noodles in the boiling water for 3 minutes.
  4. While waiting, mix the special seasoning with oil and soy sauce in a plate.
  5. After 3 minutes, drain the noodles and place it in the prepared plate.
  6. Mix it well.
  7. Remove the shell of the boiled egg and place it in the plate along with the noodles.
  8. Enjoy your food!
