Niel's Ultimate Milo

Description

Milo is a chocolate malt drink popular among children and adults here in the Philippines. This recipe will show you on how I prepare my Milo chocolate drink. Niel's ultimate milo can be paired with the Lucky Me Pancit Canton for a better dining experience.

Ingredients

  • Milo powder
  • Sugar
  • Nestle's Fresh Milk
  • Water
  • Ice cubes

Steps

  1. Put three spoonfuls of Milo powder in one glass.
  2. Put a half spoon of sugar.
  3. Fill the glass with a little amount of water until the powders are submerged.
  4. Mix it well until the powder is dissolved.
  5. Put 4 pcs of ice cubes.
  6. Fill the glass with Nestle's fresh milk until it is full.
  7. Stir well and enjoy!
